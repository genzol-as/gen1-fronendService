How To Use

Start by using the GEN1 Chain Cleaner to spray and remove dirt, grease, and grime from the chain and sprockets. Scrub lightly with a chain cleaning brush if needed, then wipe or rinse the chain clean and allow it to dry. Follow with GEN1 Foam Chain Lube, applying evenly along the chain while rotating the wheel or pedals, focusing on the rollers and pins. Wipe off any excess lube to prevent dirt accumulation. Regular use keeps the chain clean, lubricated, and protected for enhanced performance.
