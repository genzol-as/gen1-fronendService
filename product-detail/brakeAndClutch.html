Brake And Clutch Cleaner

PACK SIZE: 500ml

GEN1 Brake & Clutch Cleaner Spray is a fast-acting, high-performance cleaner designed for automotive and motorcycle brake and clutch systems. It effectively removes grease, oil, brake dust, and other contaminants, ensuring optimal performance and safety. The solvent-based formula evaporates quickly without leaving residues and is safe for metal and rubber components.

How To Use

Shake the can well and spray directly onto brake discs, pads, calipers, clutch plates, or other components while the vehicle is stationary and cool. Allow the cleaner to penetrate and dissolve contaminants, then wipe off loosened debris or let it evaporate. Avoid painted surfaces and use in a well-ventilated area.

Advantages

Removes grease, oil, and brake dust quickly and thoroughly, restoring braking and clutch efficiency. Fast-evaporating formula prevents sticking and noise, protects components, and enhances performance, reliability, and longevity of critical systems.
