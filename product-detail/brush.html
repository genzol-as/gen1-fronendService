Chain Cleaning Brush

PACK SIZE: 1 NUMBER

GEN1 Chain Cleaning Brush is a durable, high-quality tool designed for thorough cleaning of motorcycle and bicycle chains, sprockets, and other drivetrain components. Its ergonomic handle and specialized bristles effectively remove dirt, grease, and grime from hard-to-reach areas without damaging metal surfaces, ensuring optimal chain maintenance and longevity.

How To Use

Hold the brush firmly and apply it to the chain, sprockets, or gears while rotating the wheel or pedals. Scrub all sides of the chain to loosen dirt and debris. After brushing, rinse or wipe the chain clean and allow it to dry before applying chain lubricant.

Advantages

GEN1 Chain Cleaning Brush ensures deep cleaning of chains and drivetrain components, reduces abrasive wear, and enhances the effectiveness of chain lubricants. Its durable bristles and ergonomic design provide efficient, safe, and precise cleaning, extending the life and performance of chains and sprockets.
