Bike and Car Shiner

Quantity : 100ml / 250ml

GEN1 Bike & Car Shiner is a premium automotive surface care product designed to restore and enhance the appearance of motorcycles and cars. Its advanced formulation cleans, polishes, and imparts a long-lasting glossy finish on painted surfaces, plastics, and metallic parts. The product removes dust, fingerprints, light dirt, and water spots without leaving streaks, enhancing surface aesthetics while providing a subtle protective layer that preserves shine and maintains a showroom-like appearance. It is safe for use on all exterior surfaces, including paint, chrome, plastics, and fiberglass.

How To Use

Ensure the vehicle surface is cool and free from heavy dirt or debris. Shake the bottle well and spray a moderate amount of GEN1 Bike & Car Shiner directly onto the surface or onto a clean, soft microfiber cloth. Spread evenly over the desired area using gentle, circular motions. Allow a few seconds for the product to work, then buff with a dry microfiber cloth to achieve a high-gloss, streak-free finish. For optimal results, apply in sections and avoid application under direct sunlight. Regular use helps maintain a consistent shine and keeps surfaces protected from dust and minor contaminants.
