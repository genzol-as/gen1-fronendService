Who We Are

With over 5 years of industry experience, GEN1 Lubricants is a professionally managed manufacturing company specializing in high-quality aerosol lubricants and bike care products we have established ourselves as a reliable manufacturer committed to consistent quality, product performance, and customer satisfaction.

We serve a wide range of customers, including Authorized distributors, by providing commitment to continuous improvement, GEN1 Lubricants strives to build long-term partnerships based on trust and reliability.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To manufacture and supply high-performance aerosol lubricants and bike care solutions that meet industry standards, support efficient maintenance, and deliver consistent value to customers through quality, reliability, and service excellence.

Our Vision

To be globally recognized as a trusted and leading manufacturer known for product integrity, continuous innovation, and a strong customer-centric approach.
