Who We Are

Gen1 Lubricant is a next-generation engine oil brand built on precision engineering and innovation. We provide high-performance lubrication solutions designed to deliver exceptional protection, efficiency, and longevity for every engine type.

From two-wheelers to heavy-duty vehicles, our products are crafted using advanced synthetic technology, ensuring superior wear resistance, reduced friction, and optimum temperature stability.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To engineer world-class lubricants that enhance engine life, reduce emissions, and support a sustainable driving future.

Our Vision

To be a globally trusted lubricant brand recognized for quality, innovation, and environmental responsibility.
